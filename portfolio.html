Introduction

I'm Heidie Saligan — though most people know me as Hyds. I'm a Social Media Manager and content creator based in Pasig City, Philippines, helping brands show up online with content that's clear, on-brand, and worth stopping for.

My path here started in customer service and sales. For years I worked the front lines of global BPO companies — taking high-volume calls for AT&T, selling for Sprint, and managing overseas accounts at an international firm. That taught me how to listen, solve problems quickly, and build trust with people I'd never meet in person. It's the same instinct I bring to every piece of content: know the audience, and speak to what they actually need.

These days I channel that into design and technology — creating visuals and social content in Canva, building clean landing pages and web presentations, and using AI tools like Claude, ChatGPT, and Higgsfield to work faster and smarter, even building automation that handles the busywork for businesses. I love the mix of creativity and systems: making something look good and making it run well.
